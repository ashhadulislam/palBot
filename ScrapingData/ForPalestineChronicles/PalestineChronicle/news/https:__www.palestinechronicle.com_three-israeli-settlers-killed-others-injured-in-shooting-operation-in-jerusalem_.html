Three Israeli settlers were killed and 12 injured on Thursday morning in a shooting operation carried out by two Palestinian brothers at the entrance to the Ramot settlement in occupied Jerusalem, Israeli media reported. The Israeli newspaper Maariv reported that Rabbi Elimelech Wasserman was among those killed in the shooting in Jerusalem on Thursday. The Israeli police announced that the perpetrators were killed.
