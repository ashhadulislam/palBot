(Photo: video grab) Share Tweet Pin Email Share Videos posted on social media also show a large crowd, chanting “Free Palestine” as well as “From the River to the Sea.” Thousands of protesters have taken to the streets of San Francisco, calling for a ceasefire in Gaza, as the city prepares to host the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) forum. The gathering took place in the city’s downtown on Tuesday evening, with crowds chanting “Genocidal Joe, he must go,” according to local media. According to the San Francisco Standard, some speakers who addressed the demonstrators urged the US – Israel’s key supporter – to stop contributing billions of dollars in military aid and instead focus on fighting poverty in America. The APEC summit, an international forum that includes 21 nations and accounts for around 60% of global GDP, opened on Saturday and is scheduled to wrap up on Friday.
