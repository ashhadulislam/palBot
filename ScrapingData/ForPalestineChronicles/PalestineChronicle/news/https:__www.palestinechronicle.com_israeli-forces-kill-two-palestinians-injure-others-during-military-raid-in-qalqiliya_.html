Two Palestinians were killed and others were injured on Monday morning during an Israeli army raid of the city of Qalqilya, in the northern West Bank, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The bodies of the two victims – identified as Alaa Nazzal and Anas Qaraqe, were reportedly seized by the Israeli army. According to eyewitnesses, Nazzal and Qaraqe were in a car when Israeli occupation soldiers opened fire at them, killing them instantly.
