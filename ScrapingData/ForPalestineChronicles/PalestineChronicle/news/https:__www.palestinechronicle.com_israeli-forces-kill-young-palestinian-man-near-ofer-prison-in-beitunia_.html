A young Palestinian man was killed and others injured during confrontations with Israeli forces in the early hours of Thursday in front of Ofer Israeli prison in the town of Beitunia, west of Ramallah, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. 21-year-old Fadi Moayad Badran, from the village of Beit Ur al-Tahta to the west of Ramallah, died of critical gunshot wounds to the chest sustained by Israeli forces live bullets in confrontations that erupted in front of Ofer prison.
