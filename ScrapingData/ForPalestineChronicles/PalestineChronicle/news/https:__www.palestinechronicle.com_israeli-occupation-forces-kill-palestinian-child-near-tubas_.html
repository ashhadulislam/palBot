A Palestinian child from the village of Tayasir, east of Tubas, was killed after being shot by Israeli occupation forces on Tuesday morning, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Medical sources at the Turkish Tubas Governmental Hospital said that Amr Ahmed Jamil Wahdan, 14, died from the injuries he sustained after being shot in the chest by live bullets in the village of Tayasir, east of Tubas. Israeli forces detained a number of Palestinians while besieging a home in the city of Tubas, before withdrawing from the town.
