Two Palestinian children were killed by Israeli occupation forces on Wednesday morning, during their ongoing aggression against the city of Jenin and its camp, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The Palestinian Ministry of Health said that Adam Samer Al-Ghoul, 9, and Basil Suleiman Abu Al-Wafa, 15, were killed as a result of being shot by occupation forces in the city of Jenin.
