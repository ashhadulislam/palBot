Israeli occupation forces killed five Palestinians during a military raid in the city and refugee camp of Jenin, north of the occupied West Bank, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Eyewitnesses reported that an Israeli military drone targeted a house in the Damaj neighborhood within the Jenin refugee camp, using at least one missile. This led to the killing of Asaad Ali Damaj, a 33-year-old man, and left 11 others injured, including three women. Furthermore, Israeli drones targeted the Public Service Center in the camp. Eyewitnesses further stated that Israeli infantry troops stopped an ambulance transporting the wounded from the refugee camp and arrested two wounded individuals who were onboard. In total, 14 Palestinians were arrested during the Israeli raid. AL-JAZEERA: Israeli forces carried out a strike in Jenin which resulted in the killing of five Palestinians. The Israeli aggression ended up in the morning with five casualties, including a child, and many injuries from live ammunition.
