A young Palestinian man succumbed on Thursday evening to injuries inflicted by Israeli army gunfire near the village of Atuf in the northern Jordan Valley, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The Palestinian Ministry of Health confirmed the murder of 25-year-old Karam Bani Odeh, who was shot by Israeli occupation forces near the village of Tammoun, in the northern Jordan Valley.
