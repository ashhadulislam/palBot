The number of Palestinians killed by the Israeli military as well as settlers in the occupied West Bank since October 7 has exceeded the number of Palestinians killed in the last year, according to the Palestinian Health Ministry. The number of Palestinians killed in the West Bank since October 7 accounts for 48% of all Palestinian fatalities in the West Bank in 2023 (452), according to an OCHA statement published on November 23. The statement says that about 66% of the fatalities since October 7 have occurred during confrontations that followed Israeli search-and-arrest operations, primarily in Jenin and Tulkarm governorates; 24% have been in the context of demonstrations concerning Gaza; seven percent have been killed while attacking or allegedly attacking Israeli forces or settlers; two percent have been killed in settler attacks against Palestinians; and one percent during punitive demolitions.
