A young Palestinian man was shot and killed on Monday morning by Israeli soldiers near the Arroub refugee camp near the southern West Bank city of Hebron (Al-Khalil), the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. According to WAFA, Israeli soldiers opened fire at a car with two people in it, hitting Mohammad Adel Sarahin, 21, from the town of Beit Ola, northwest of Hebron. Israeli soldiers reportedly prevented ambulances from approaching Sarahin while he was left bleeding to death.
