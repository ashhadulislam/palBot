Israeli occupation forces carried out a military raid on Jenin on Tuesday night, which continued for the second day on Wednesday, amid reports of explosions, gunfire, and clashes that injured six people, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The Israeli army declared the city of Jenin and its camp a closed military zone and deployed its forces with large reinforcements in several neighborhoods.
