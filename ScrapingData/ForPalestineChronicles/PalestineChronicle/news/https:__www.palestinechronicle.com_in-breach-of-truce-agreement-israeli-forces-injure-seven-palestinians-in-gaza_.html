In a breach of the humanitarian truce agreement, seven Palestinian civilians were injured on Sunday by Israeli occupation forces’ gunfire in two separate incidents in front of the Al-Quds and Indonesian hospitals in the northern Gaza Strip, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. WAFA correspondent said that Israeli occupation forces opened fire on a group of civilians who were inspecting their homes around the Indonesian Hospital in the town of Beit Lahiya, north of the Gaza Strip.
