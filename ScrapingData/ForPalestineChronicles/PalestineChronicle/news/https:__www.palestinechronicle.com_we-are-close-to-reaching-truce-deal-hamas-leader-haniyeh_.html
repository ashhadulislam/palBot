The head of Hamas’ political bureau, Ismail Haniyeh, said on Tuesday that the Palestinian Resistance movement is near a truce agreement with Israel, Reuters news agency reported. “Hamas officials are ‘close to reaching a truce agreement’ with Israel and the group has delivered its response to Qatari mediators, Ismail Haniyeh said in a statement sent to Reuters by his aide,” the news agency wrote. Izzat al-Risheq, a member of the political bureau of Hamas confirmed the news to Al-Jazeera channel, explaining that the details of the truce agreement will be revealed in the coming hours. “Now, the issue is up to the brothers in Qatar, they will announce the truce agreement with Israel including its terms,” Izzat al-Risheq said.
