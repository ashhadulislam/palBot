Now her father says that she was re-arrested. In clear violation of the terms for the exchange of prisoners, the Israeli occupation authorities re-arrested the child prisoner, Nofoth Hammad, 16, after she was supposed to be released on Friday, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Nofooth Hammad, 16, was supposed to be released from Israeli prisons as part of the truce agreement between the Palestinian Resistance and the Israeli government but she has never arrived home, according to her father Jad Hammad.
