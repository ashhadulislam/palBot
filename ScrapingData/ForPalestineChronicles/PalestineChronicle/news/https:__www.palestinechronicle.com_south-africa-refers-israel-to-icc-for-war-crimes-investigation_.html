(Photo: Kremlin, via Wikimedia Commons) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff The South African government wants the International Criminal Court to investigate Israel for war crimes committed in its ongoing assault on the Gaza Strip. Cyril Ramaphosa, the SA president, told a press conference in Doha on Wednesday that “Gaza has now turned into a concentration camp where genocide is taking place”. “As South Africa we have accordingly, together with many other countries in the world, saw fit to refer this whole Israeli government action to the ICC. We have put through a referral because we believe that war crimes are being committed there,” he said. Ramaphosa said his government is opposed to the ongoing operation, “particularly as it is now targeting hospitals where babies, women and the injured are dying… And where care of life has been completely ignored.” SOUTH AFRICA PRESIDENT: "We have put through a referral to the ICC because we believe that war crimes have been committed there.
