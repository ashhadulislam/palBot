Dozens of Palestinians were killed and injured as Israel continued to bomb hospitals and schools sheltering displaced people, as well as homes and buildings, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Northern Gaza After destroying the Al-Shifa Medical Complex in Gaza City and putting it totally out of service, the Israeli forces surrounded at dawn on Monday the Indonesian Hospital in the northern Gaza Strip, targeting it with artillery shelling, which led to the death of at least 12 wounded people who were receiving treatment. Two doctors were also reportedly wounded. At dawn, the Israeli artillery bombed the Kuwait School, near the Indonesian Hospital, in the northern Gaza Strip. Local sources reported that Israeli artillery and tanks, which encircled the Indonesian Hospital and bombarded its surroundings since the early hours of the night, fired several shells toward the Kuwait School, which is sheltering a number of displaced people.
