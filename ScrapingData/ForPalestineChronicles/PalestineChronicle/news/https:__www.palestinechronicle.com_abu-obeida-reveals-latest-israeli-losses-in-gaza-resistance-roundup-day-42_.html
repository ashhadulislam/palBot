In his latest statement, Abu Obaida, the military spokesman for the Al-Qassam Brigades, the armed wing of the Palestinian Resistance Movement Hamas, summarized Israel’s military losses in recent days. He also commented on the Israeli takeover of the Al-Shifa Hospital in Gaza, among other issues. In a just-released video, Al-Qassam Brigades fighters are seen neutralizing an Israeli special force stationed inside a building in Beit Hanoun with machine guns and 12 anti-fortifications and anti-armor shells.
