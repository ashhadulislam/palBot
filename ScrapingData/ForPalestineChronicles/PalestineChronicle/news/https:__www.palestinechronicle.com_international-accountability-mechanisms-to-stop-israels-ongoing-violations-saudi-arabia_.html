(Photo: File) Share Tweet Pin Email Share “The Kingdom stresses the necessity of activating international accountability mechanisms regarding these ongoing violations by the Israeli occupation forces” – Saudi Arabia Saudi Arabia today condemned on Thursday Israel’s storming of Al-Shifa Medical Complex and the bombing of the Jordanian Field Hospital in the Gaza Strip, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. “The Foreign Ministry expresses the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia’s strong condemnation and rejection of the Israeli occupation forces storming Al-Shifa Medical Hospital and bombing the vicinity of the Jordanian Field Hospital in the besieged Gaza Strip,” the Saudi Foreign Ministry said in a statement. This is “a blatant violation of international humanitarian law, all international norms and conventions, and explicit targeting of civilians and medical personnel,” the statement added.
