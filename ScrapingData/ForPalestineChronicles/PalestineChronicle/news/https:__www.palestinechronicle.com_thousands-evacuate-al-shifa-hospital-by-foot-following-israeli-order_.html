The Israeli army began evacuating the Al-Shifa Medical Complex in Gaza City on Saturday morning after expelling the wounded and displaced people from it, Al-Jazeera reported. The Director General of the Ministry of Health in Gaza, Munir al-Bursh, told Al-Jazeera that only five doctors are still in the complex to supervise the evacuation of the wounded patients. Al-Jazeera initially reported that 120 wounded patients, out of 650, along with the premature babies, are still inside the hospital, and the medical staff is coordinating with the Red Cross regarding them.
