The Government Media Office in Gaza said on Monday evening that Israeli authorities had extended the detention of the director of Gaza’s Al-Shifa Hospital for an additional 45 days, the Middle East Monitor reported. “The occupation army extended the detention of the director of Al-Shifa Hospital in Gaza for 45 days pending investigation,” its statement said.
