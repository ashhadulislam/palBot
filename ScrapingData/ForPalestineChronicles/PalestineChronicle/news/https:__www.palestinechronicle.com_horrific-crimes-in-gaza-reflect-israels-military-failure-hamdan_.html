In a press conference in Beirut, Lebanon, Hamdan stated that Israel’s many massacres against innocent civilians are indicative of its vindictive nature, and the reflection of its failure on the battlefield. Below is a summary of Hamdan’s press statement. ‘Nazi Massacres’ – The occupation’s horrific massacres against defenseless civilians reveal its retaliatory behavior due to the extent of (its) losses. – The Nazi massacres are not limited only to Gaza, but also extended to the West Bank and Al-Quds. – There are no safe areas in all of the Gaza Strip, despite American claims and the propaganda of the occupation.
