The relentless Israeli bombardments have resulted in the destruction of numerous homes, buildings, residential apartments, and both public and private properties. In Gaza City, several civilians were killed and others injured when an Israeli airstrike targeted a residential building in the Al-Daraj neighborhood, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported.
