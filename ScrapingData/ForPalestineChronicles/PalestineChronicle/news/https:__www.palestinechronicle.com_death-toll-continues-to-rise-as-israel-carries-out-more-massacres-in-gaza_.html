Dozens of Palestinians were murdered and many others injured on Sunday as the Israeli genocide campaign in Gaza enters its 44th day, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. 14 Palestinian civilians were killed on Sunday after Israeli warplanes bombed two houses in the Nuseirat camp in the central Gaza Strip.
