(Photo: Waleed Musleh, via Social Media) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff Dozens of Palestinians were killed and others injured on Wednesday night and Thursday morning as a result of a series of Israeli airstrikes on various areas in the Gaza Strip. Israeli warplanes bombed a petrol station in central Gaza and a mosque in Khan Yunis, south of the Gaza Strip, resulting in nine deaths and dozens of injuries. Around 50 Palestinians were killed and dozens of others injured on Wednesday night in a new massacre carried out by Israeli warplanes in the Sabra neighborhood in central Gaza. The official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported that the Israeli warplanes targeted a mosque in the Sabra neighborhood, killing around 50 people, in addition to dozens of wounded.
