(Photo: Rafael Stedile, via Peoplesdispatch.org) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff The South African government intends to petition the International Criminal Court and the International Court of Justice to declare Israel an apartheid state. According to Naledi Pandor, the Minister of International Relations and Cooperation, South Africa and Palestine were currently working on formulating political strategies towards taking up the Palestinian cause to the ICC and ICJ, to “declare Israel an apartheid state”. This follows the country’s referral to the ICC last week to investigate Israel for war crimes committed during its onslaught on the Gaza Strip.
