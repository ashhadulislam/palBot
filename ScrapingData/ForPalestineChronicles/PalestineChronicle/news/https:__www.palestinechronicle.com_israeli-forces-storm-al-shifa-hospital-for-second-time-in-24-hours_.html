Israeli forces stormed the Al-Shifa Hospital for a second time in 24 hours. (Photo: video grab) Share Tweet Pin Email Share Israeli occupation forces remained inside the Al-Shifa Medical Complex, west of Gaza City, after storming it for the second time in 24 hours, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Local sources told WAFA that Israeli bulldozers and tanks raided the medical complex from its western entrance. The Israeli tanks have been surrounding the Al-Shifa hospital with military tanks for the past week. On Wednesday night, the Israeli occupation forces had withdrawn from inside Al-Shifa Medical Complex after storming it at dawn. The raids on the hospital departments were carried out gradually and the occupation army blew up the doors between the departments while ordering all those in the medical complex to gather at the center of its eastern courtyard, WAFA correspondent said.
