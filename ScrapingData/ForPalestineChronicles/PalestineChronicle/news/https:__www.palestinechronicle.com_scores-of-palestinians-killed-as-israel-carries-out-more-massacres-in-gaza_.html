(Photo: via Social Media) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff Israeli warplanes attacked different locations in the Gaza Strip on Wednesday night and Thursday morning, killing dozens of Palestinians and wounding many more. On Thursday morning, Israeli forces launched several raids on residential buildings in the Nuseirat refugee camp, in central Gaza, and in Jabaliya, in northern Gaza, killing and injuring dozens. WAFA correspondent said that photojournalist Mohammad Moin Ayyash was killed, along with a number of his family members after Israeli warplanes bombed his house in Nuseirat. More raids were launched on residential buildings in Nuseirat, resulting in the killing and injuring of dozens of civilians.
