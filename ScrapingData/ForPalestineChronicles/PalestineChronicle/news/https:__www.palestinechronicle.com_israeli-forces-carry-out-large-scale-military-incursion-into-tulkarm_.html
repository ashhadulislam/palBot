Israeli occupation forces carried out a large-scale military incursion into the city of Tulkarem and its refugee camp in the early hours of Thursday, causing heavy destruction to the streets, vehicles, and property, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. WAFA correspondent said that Israeli occupation forces, accompanied by a bulldozer, stormed the city from the western side and patrolled their troops in its streets and neighborhoods, particularly the southern and eastern parts.
