For the second day in a row, Israeli occupation forces continued on Friday their strict siege of the town of Aqraba, located south of Nablus in the occupied West Bank, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. Salah Jaber, the mayor of Aqraba, told WAFA that Israeli occupation forces are still enforcing a blockade on the town, having sealed off its entrances, and preventing residents from entering or leaving.
