In another Israeli breach of the humanitarian truce agreement in the Gaza Strip, a farmer was killed and another injured on Sunday morning by Israeli occupation forces in the central Gaza Strip, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The Palestinian Red Crescent Society (PRCS) said in a press statement that Israeli occupation forces targeted two farmers while they were working on their land east of the Al-Maghazi refugee camp in the central governorate of Gaza. The attack resulted in the killing of one of them and the injury of the other.
