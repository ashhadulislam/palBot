(Photo: Mahmoud Ajjour, The Palestine Chronicle) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff The Al-Qassam Brigades, the military wing of the Palestinian Resistance Movement Hamas, announced on Sunday an operation targeting a large number of Israeli invading soldiers in Gaza. According to a statement released by Al-Qassam, their fighters managed to locate 60 soldiers who were positioned east of Juhr al-Dik, south of Gaza City. The fighters reportedly targeted the invading Israeli forces with three anti-personnel devices and they withdrew to their positions safely, after killing and wounding a large number of Israeli soldiers.
