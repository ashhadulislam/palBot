More than a hundred innocent Palestinian civilians were brutally murdered on Saturday in an Israeli massacre targeting a residential building in Jabaliya, north of the Gaza Strip, the official Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. The building housed hundreds of civilians as a result of the ongoing Israeli genocide. The residential building, belonging to the Obeid family in Jabaliya, was targeted by a massively huge Israeli missile strike.
