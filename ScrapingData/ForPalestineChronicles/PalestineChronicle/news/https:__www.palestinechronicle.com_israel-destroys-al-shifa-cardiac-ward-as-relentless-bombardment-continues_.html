(Photo: Mahmoud Ajjour, The Palestine Chronicle) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff An Israeli airstrike destroyed the cardiac ward at Gaza’s Al Shifa Medical Complex in the Gaza Strip, according to a Palestinian official. Munir al-Bursh, the director-general of the pharmaceutical department at the Gaza Health Ministry, told reporters that the Al-Shifa Complex was subjected to Israeli shelling, and its cardiology department destroyed. Relentless Bombardment and Siege The Israeli military continues its relentless bombardment and military siege of the largest hospital in the Strip, amid warnings of an imminent humanitarian catastrophe in the complex where thousands of patients and wounded and displaced people are trapped. A spokesman for the Ministry of Health in Gaza told Al-Jazeera that doctors are unable to provide any life-saving service to the wounded and sick in the Al-Shifa Complex. The spokesman added that two premature babies and a patient in intensive care already died due to the lack of oxygen and that they expect more to die.
