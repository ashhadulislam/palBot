(Photo: Mahmoud Ajjour, The Palestine Chronicle) Share Tweet Pin Email Share By Palestine Chronicle Staff The Israeli army said on Tuesday that its warplanes bombed about 200 targets in Gaza last night. The airstrikes were carried out in the Al-Rimal suburb and in Khan Yunis. The Palestinian Ministry of Health said in a statement that the number of Palestinians killed had risen to 788 and the number of wounded to 4,100 as a result of Israeli strikes since the start of the Al-Aqsa Flood operation.
