ABOUT

Palestine Remix puts dozens of documentaries on Palestine at your fingertips, allowing you to view or use parts of various films to create your own productions.

 

Using cutting-edge technology that synchronises text and video, you can instantly find anything mentioned in the films.

 

Explore the modern history of Palestine in an easy-to-use format. Click on any year in our timeline to discover the storyâs milestones and major players. Watch amazing drone footage high above historic Palestinian towns. Explore a data visualization that highlights how and when hundreds of villages were destroyed, turning hundreds of thousands of Palestinians into refugees overnight. Vanishing Palestine is an interactive map that summarizes in one minute how Palestine was colonized â and what the land looks like today.

 

Palestine Remix is your interactive resource to learn about the little-known narratives of the Palestinian-Israeli story.

 

You can explore Palestine Remix in any of the four different languages in which these films are available: English, Arabic, Turkish and Bosnian.

 

We have made our documentaries open and free to be used as you wish.

 

Happy remixing!
